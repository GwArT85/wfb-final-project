Tekken 5 is one of my favorite PS2 fighting games.

This game brought back many of the characters that had been missing in Tekken 4. It is very fun to juggle characters in the game and you have a huge selection to choose from. I give this game a 9 out of 10 as this is a good game with fun modes, but it has been replaced by games that have been released since then with better mechanics.
