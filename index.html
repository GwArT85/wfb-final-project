Retro Gamespace

Replaying my all-time favorite retro games.

Nostalgia may be fleeting, but the fun sure isn't!

The PS1/PS2 were some of my main sources of entertainment for basically the first nine years of my life. I then moved on to the PS3 around the time my PS2 stopped working. Years later I found out about a program called PCSX2 which allows you to emulate PS2 games on your PC, provided you have a powerful enough computer along with the original bios files and game discs. Here I will write about my experiences re-experiencing those classic games such as NFL Street 1 & 3, Tekken 4 and Tekken 5.
